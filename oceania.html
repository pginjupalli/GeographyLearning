Fiji:

    Location: South Pacific
  • Capital City: Suva
  • Population: 929,766
  • Common Language(s): English, Fijian, Hindi
  • Religions/Beliefs: Christianity, Hinduism

Fiji is renowned for its rich culture, influenced by Indian heritages and a mix of European and Asian cultures. This amalgamation is reflected in its strong emphasis on communal living known as "vanua." The economy of Fiji relies on agriculture, tourism, and remittances, with tourism being a major contributor. Politically, Fiji has experienced periods of political instability, including military coups; however, it has since transitioned to a more stable democratic system.

  • Capital City: Port Vila
